About EUG-CON 2017

Mark your calendar: the 2017 Eugene Electronic Music Conference is coming this summer, July 17 - 20.

Bringing together some of the America's leading figures in electronic music for a weekend of learning, sharing, and jamming, EUG-CON is designed to enrich and inspire the Pacific Northwest's electronic music community.

Artists in Attendance

We will be announcing our list of performers and guest artists soon. Stay tuned.

Get together with the leading members of the Northwest electronic music community and share skills with some of the area's most influential beat-makers. See the workflow of professional studio musicians.

EUG-CON is committed to being inclusive and welcoming for everyone. We look forward to an intensive and fun weekend of bleeps and bloops.
